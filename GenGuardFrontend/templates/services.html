About our system

The journey begins with the user's registration. Once registered, our system, through GenGuard, initiates a seamless integration process where each user is assigned a unique QR code, linked directly to their profile, which serves as their digital key. Which is our first layer of security. Upon successful registration & approval by the admin, this QR can be scanned by the QR scanner for the next step of our security layer.

Scanning for Access: To gain entry, the user simply scans their QR code at the designated Sall-E scanner located at the entrance. This action triggers the system's authentication process.

Facial Recognition Authentication: As the QR code is scanned, the Sall-E system activates its advanced facial recognition technology, comparing the user's live image captured by the on-site cameras with the profile picture stored in our secure database.

Access Granted: If the system confirms a match between the live image and the database profile picture, access is granted. This verification process ensures that only registered and authorized users can enter the secured zones.

Through Sall-E and GenGuard, we're not just controlling access; we're redefining security and efficiency. Our system's dual-layer authentication process, combining QR codes and facial recognition, ensures that your facility is safeguarded against unauthorized access, providing peace of mind and a secure environment for everyone involved.
